Maslow's Hierarchy of Needs: Team Motivation MCQ

1. A team member is concerned about job security due to recent layoffs. Which level of Maslow’s hierarchy is most relevant?

Answer: b) Safety Needs
Explanation: Job security relates to Safety Needs, the second level in Maslow’s hierarchy, which includes stability, safety, and financial security. Addressing these concerns can motivate the team member by alleviating fears about their job.

2. A manager offers competitive salaries to ensure employees can afford basic necessities. Which need is being addressed?

Answer: a) Physiological Needs
Explanation: Physiological Needs, the base of Maslow’s hierarchy, include essentials like food, water, and shelter. Competitive salaries ensure employees can meet these basic needs, forming the foundation for motivation.

3. A team feels disconnected and lacks collaboration. What should a manager focus on to improve motivation?

Answer: b) Belongingness Needs
Explanation: Belongingness Needs involve social connections and teamwork. Organizing team-building activities or fostering collaboration can address this level, enhancing team motivation.

4. An employee seeks recognition for their innovative project contributions. Which need is most relevant?

Answer: c) Esteem Needs
Explanation: Esteem Needs include recognition and respect. Acknowledging the employee’s contributions through awards or praise can boost their motivation at this level.

5. A team member wants to pursue a challenging project to reach their full potential. Which level is this?

Answer: d) Self-Actualization Needs
Explanation: Self-Actualization, the top of Maslow’s hierarchy, involves achieving personal growth and potential. Providing challenging projects can motivate employees at this level.

6. A company provides health insurance to its employees. Which need is being addressed?

Answer: b) Safety Needs
Explanation: Health insurance addresses Safety Needs by ensuring employees’ health and financial stability in case of medical issues, enhancing their sense of security.

7. A manager organizes a team outing to improve morale. Which need is targeted?

Answer: c) Belongingness Needs
Explanation: A team outing fosters social bonds, addressing Belongingness Needs. This strengthens team cohesion and boosts motivation through improved relationships.

8. An employee feels unmotivated due to lack of career growth opportunities. Which need should be addressed?

Answer: d) Self-Actualization Needs
Explanation: Career growth opportunities align with Self-Actualization Needs, as they allow employees to achieve their full potential and find fulfillment in their work.

9. A company installs ergonomic chairs to improve workplace comfort. Which need is addressed?

Answer: a) Physiological Needs
Explanation: Ergonomic chairs improve physical comfort, addressing Physiological Needs by ensuring employees’ basic requirements for a comfortable work environment are met.

10. A team member is demotivated due to lack of respect from peers. Which need is unfulfilled?

Answer: d) Esteem Needs
Explanation: Lack of respect impacts Esteem Needs, as employees seek recognition and respect from peers. Addressing this through a positive team culture can enhance motivation.
